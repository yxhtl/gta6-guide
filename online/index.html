GTA6 Online Preview

GTA6 Online will be a completely fresh start — every player begins from zero. No decade-old accounts, no max-level players griefing newbies in jets. The first three months of GTA6 Online will be the most exciting period in the GTA community in years.

What Rockstar has hinted at:

  • Dynamic map — GTA6's world will evolve over time, with new buildings, events, and storylines (similar to Fortnite's seasonal map updates)
  • Cross-play — at minimum between PlayStation and Xbox, with PC possibly included
  • Roleplay features — Rockstar acquired Cfx.re (the largest GTA RP server team and FiveM developers) in 2023, suggesting built-in RP mechanics
  • Dedicated servers — goodbye to GTA5 Online's P2P connections (high latency, rampant cheating)
