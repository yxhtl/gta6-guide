Vigilante

Chapter: Chapter 2 · Difficulty: Normal · Reward: $500-$2000 per level

Walkthrough Steps

  1. Steal any police car.
  2. Press the horn to activate vigilante mode.
  3. Chase and eliminate the target criminals.
  4. Each level adds more targets. Survive to level 12 for the armor bonus.

Trivia

The original Vice City vigilante rewarded you with 150 armor after level 12 — expect a similar bonus in GTA6.

Pro Tips

The police car's sirens clear traffic ahead of you — use them on busy streets. Helicopters make vigilante much easier.
