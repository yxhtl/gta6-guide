Street Racer

Chapter: Chapter 2 · Difficulty: Normal · Reward: $2000-$5000 per race

Mission Objectives

  1. Find a race marker (checkered flag icon).
  2. Pay the entry fee and start the race.
  3. Beat the other racers to the finish line.
  4. Win races to unlock faster cars and higher-stakes events.

Tips

Use a car with high acceleration for city races (tight corners) and high top speed for highway races. The Sanchez dirt bike dominates off-road races.

Trivia

Street racing is GTA6's version of GTA5's street race side activity. The best racers in Vice City hang out at the Vice Beach parking lot at midnight.
