Assassination Contracts

Chapter: Chapter 3 · Difficulty: Hard · Reward: $5000-$25000 per contract

Walkthrough Steps

  1. Find a ringing payphone (random spawn).
  2. Answer the call to receive the contract.
  3. Eliminate the target using the specified method.
  4. Some contracts require specific weapons or accident kills for bonus pay.

Trivia

Payphone assassinations first appeared in GTA3 and returned in GTA Online's "The Contract" update. GTA6 brings them back with more variety.

Pro Tips

Read the contract details carefully — using the wrong weapon halves your pay. Sniping from rooftops is the safest approach for most targets.
