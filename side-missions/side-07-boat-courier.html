Boat Courier

Chapter: Chapter 2 · Difficulty: Easy · Reward: $800-$3000 per run

Mission Objectives

  1. Steal or buy a boat.
  2. Find the courier icon at any dock.
  3. Pick up the package and deliver it across the water.
  4. Avoid coast guard patrols — they'll chase you if you get too close.

Tips

The Squalo is the best boat for courier work — fast and stable. Stay in open water rather than narrow channels to avoid patrol boats.

Trivia

Vice City's massive coastline and multiple islands make boat missions a natural fit. The original Vice City had boat checkpoint missions that were fan favorites.
