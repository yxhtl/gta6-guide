Firefighter

Chapter: Chapter 1 · Difficulty: Easy · Reward: $400-$1200 per level

This side mission is available from Chapter 1 and is rated Easy. Completing it rewards you with $400-$1200 per level.

What You Need to Do

  1. Steal a fire truck.
  2. Activate firefighter mode.
  3. Drive to the burning vehicle and use the water cannon to extinguish it.
  4. Put out all fires in the area to complete the level. Fireproof bonus at level 12.

Strategy Guide

The water cannon has surprising range — you don't need to be right next to the fire. Stay back and spray.

Trivia

Completing firefighter level 12 makes you fireproof in every GTA game. Essential for the flamethrower missions later.
