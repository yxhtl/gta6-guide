Taxi Driver

Chapter: Chapter 1 · Difficulty: Easy · Reward: $200-$500 per fare

Mission Objectives

  1. Find a taxi or steal one.
  2. Press the horn button to start taxi mode.
  3. Pick up passengers (blue blip on map).
  4. Deliver them to the destination quickly for a tip bonus.
  5. Complete 50 fares in a row for the taxi unlock bonus.

Tips

Use the cinematic camera during drives — passengers tip more for smooth driving. Avoid collisions to keep the tip multiplier up.

Trivia

Taxi missions have been in every GTA since GTA3. In Vice City (2002) you unlocked the Zebra Cab after 100 fares.
