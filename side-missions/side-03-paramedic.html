Paramedic

Chapter: Chapter 1 · Difficulty: Easy · Reward: $300-$1500 per level

This side mission is available from Chapter 1 and is rated Easy. Completing it rewards you with $300-$1500 per level.

What You Need to Do

  1. Steal an ambulance.
  2. Activate paramedic mode (horn button).
  3. Pick up patients and deliver them to the hospital.
  4. Be careful — patients die if you crash too hard. Complete all 12 levels for the health bonus.

Strategy Guide

Plan your route before picking up patients. The hardest part is the time limit on later levels with more patients.

Trivia

Paramedic level 12 is infamous among GTA veterans — it takes 45+ minutes of flawless driving. Save before attempting.
