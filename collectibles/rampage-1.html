Rampage #1

Rampage · Collectibles

Series staple since GTA3. Kill a target number of enemies within a time limit.

Area
Vice Port
Type
Rampage
Reward
$800

Where to Find It

Destroy 15 vehicles in 2 minutes at the container yard near the cargo cranes.

Tips

Use the RPG or grenades — you need area damage to hit the vehicle count. Spawns at the red skull icon.
