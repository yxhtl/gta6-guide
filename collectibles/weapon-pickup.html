Weapon Pickup

Weapon Pickup · Collectibles
Area
Starfish Island
Type
Weapon Pickup
Reward
Free RPG

Rare weapon spawn that respawns every 48 in-game hours. Worth revisiting.

How to Get It

Behind the mansion pool house on the east side of the island. Next to the hedge maze.

Tips

Weapon pickups respawn — mark this on your map and revisit whenever you need free RPG ammo.
