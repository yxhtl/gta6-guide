Stunt Jump #3

Unique Stunt Jump · Collectibles

High-speed jump near the airport. Needs a very fast car to clear the distance.

Area
Airport Perimeter
Type
Unique Stunt Jump
Reward
$2000

Where to Find It

The dirt mound at the end of the airport runway access road. Jump over the security fence.

Tips

A fully upgraded sports car is recommended. Anything under 8.0 speed will fall short.
