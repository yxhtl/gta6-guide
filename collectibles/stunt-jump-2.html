Stunt Jump #2

Unique Stunt Jump · Collectibles

One of the harder stunt jumps. Requires precise timing with the drawbridge mechanic.

Area
Downtown Bridge
Type
Unique Stunt Jump
Reward
$1500

Where to Find It

Jump off the raised drawbridge when it opens for a cargo ship. Land on the ship's deck.

Tips

Wait at the bridge control booth until a ship approaches — the bridge raises automatically.
