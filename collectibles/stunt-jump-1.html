Stunt Jump #1

Unique Stunt Jump · Collectibles
Area
Vice Beach Boardwalk
Type
Unique Stunt Jump
Reward
$1000

Unique stunt jumps require you to launch off a ramp and land in a specific zone. GTA5 had 50 of these.

How to Get It

Ramp at the north end of the boardwalk. Hit it at full speed and clear the pier.

Tips

Use a fast motorcycle for stunt jumps — they accelerate quicker than cars on short run-ups.
