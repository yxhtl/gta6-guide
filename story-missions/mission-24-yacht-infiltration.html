Yacht Infiltration

Chapter: Chapter 4: The Big Time · Difficulty: Normal · Reward: $14,000

Mission Objectives

  1. Take a jetski to the yacht party offshore
  2. Find and photograph the three incriminating documents
  3. Upload them to your contact via phone
  4. Leave the yacht without raising any alarm

Tips

Dress code matters — wear the outfit provided during the setup mission. If you fall in the water, you'll need to change clothes or the guards get suspicious of your wet appearance.

Trivia

Social stealth — blending into crowds and events — is expected to be a significant new mechanic in GTA6, inspired by Red Dead Redemption 2's witness and disguise systems.
