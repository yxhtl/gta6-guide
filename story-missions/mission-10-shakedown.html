Shakedown

Chapter: Chapter 1: Making Contact · Difficulty: Easy · Reward: $2,500 + SMG

Mission Objectives

  1. Visit five businesses in Little Haiti
  2. Collect payment from each business owner
  3. Deal with the one refusing to pay
  4. Return to your crew's base

Tips

Start with the northern businesses — the southern ones get spooked and are harder to collect from. Breaking one window is usually enough to persuade the holdout.

Trivia

Protection racket missions are a Vice City tradition, going back to the original GTA: Vice City's asset system and gang territory mechanics.
