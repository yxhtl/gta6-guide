Cartel Connection

Chapter: Chapter 3: Power Struggle · Difficulty: Normal · Reward: $12,000

Walkthrough Steps

  1. Travel to the cartel boss's oceanfront mansion
  2. Complete three tasks to earn their trust — Race: win the street race
  3. Collection: repossess the stolen sports car
  4. Enforcement: eliminate the snitch before he testifies

Trivia

The branching faction reputation system is one of the most anticipated features in GTA6, potentially affecting which missions are available and how the story unfolds.

Pro Tips

Choices in earlier missions can affect your cartel trust level. Higher trust means better rewards but makes it harder to betray them later.
