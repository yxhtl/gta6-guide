Federal Reserve

Chapter: Chapter 5: Endgame · Difficulty: Very Hard · Reward: $50,000 - $80,000

This mission takes place in Chapter 5: Endgame and is rated Very Hard. Completing it rewards you with $50,000 - $80,000.

What You Need to Do

  1. Drill into the underground vault from the storm drain
  2. Manage the thermal drill while waves of cops attack
  3. Load the gold bars into the freight elevator
  4. Escape through the subway tunnel network
  5. Surface at the designated extraction point
  6. Drive to the buyer's warehouse to fence the gold

Strategy Guide

The drill attracts waves of cops — set up proximity mines in the tunnel approaches beforehand. The subway escape has three possible routes; use the one you scouted during the setup mission for the smoothest exit.

Trivia

The Federal Reserve heist is expected to be GTA6's equivalent of GTA5's Union Depository — the single biggest score in the entire game.
