The Cuban Job

Chapter: Chapter 2: Rising Heat · Difficulty: Normal · Reward: $7,000

This mission takes place in Chapter 2: Rising Heat and is rated Normal. Completing it rewards you with $7,000.

What You Need to Do

  1. Rendezvous with the Cuban contact in Little Havana
  2. Transport the merchandise across town
  3. Survive the gang ambush in the industrial district
  4. Deliver to the warehouse

Strategy Guide

Don't stop for the ambush — keep driving and let your passenger shoot. If the vehicle takes too much damage, swap cars quickly in the alley.

Trivia

Cuban and Haitian gang dynamics were a major theme in the original GTA: Vice City and are expected to return in GTA6, reflecting Miami's real cultural landscape.
