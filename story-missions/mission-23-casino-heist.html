Casino Heist

Chapter: Chapter 4: The Big Time · Difficulty: Hard · Reward: $25,000 - $50,000

This mission takes place in Chapter 4: The Big Time and is rated Hard. Completing it rewards you with $25,000 - $50,000.

What You Need to Do

  1. Scout the casino (pose as a high-roller guest)
  2. Choose your approach — stealth (hack the vault) or loud (blast through)
  3. Complete 2-3 setup missions based on your chosen approach
  4. Execute the heist
  5. Escape through the maintenance tunnels

Strategy Guide

The stealth approach pays more but requires more setup missions. The loud approach is faster but you keep a smaller cut. Scout thoroughly — hidden entrances exist on all four sides of the casino.

Trivia

Heists are expected to be GTA6's flagship feature, greatly expanded from GTA5's five-heist structure. Multiple approaches, dynamic loot values, and crew management are anticipated.
