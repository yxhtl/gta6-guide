Dirty Cop

Chapter: Chapter 2: Rising Heat · Difficulty: Normal · Reward: $8,500 + Police Uniform

This mission takes place in Chapter 2: Rising Heat and is rated Normal. Completing it rewards you with $8,500 + Police Uniform.

What You Need to Do

  1. Meet the corrupt IAB contact at the precinct
  2. Plant evidence in the target's locker
  3. Frame him during the subsequent raid
  4. Escape the internal affairs investigation

Strategy Guide

Change into the police uniform to move freely inside the station. Don't run — walking speed raises less suspicion. Avoid eye contact with the desk sergeant.

Trivia

Corrupt police storylines are a hallmark of GTA narratives, from Officer Tenpenny in San Andreas to Francis McReary in GTA4.
