Heat

Chapter: Chapter 2 · Difficulty: Hard · Reward: $5000

Walkthrough Steps

  1. Ambush the armored truck at the bridge.
  2. Fight through SWAT response.
  3. Escape through the Everglades.
  4. Switch vehicles at the bait car location.
  5. Lose 5-star wanted level.

Trivia

The 5-star escape sequence is the first major difficulty spike — many players will need multiple attempts.

Pro Tips

SWAT uses tear gas — stay mobile and avoid confined spaces. Use water to lose helicopters in the swamp.
