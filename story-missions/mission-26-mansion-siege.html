Mansion Siege

Chapter: Chapter 4: The Big Time · Difficulty: Very Hard · Reward: $18,000 + RPG

Walkthrough Steps

  1. Assemble the crew at the staging point
  2. Approach the rival kingpin's oceanfront mansion
  3. Suppress the exterior guards (watch for snipers on the roof)
  4. Breach the front gate with C4 or a battering ram
  5. Clear the mansion room by room
  6. Confront the rival boss (player choice: kill or spare)

Trivia

Mansion interiors are expected to be fully explorable without loading screens, a significant technical leap from GTA5 where most interiors were separate instances.

Pro Tips

Bring heavy weapons — the mansion has RPG-wielding guards on the upper floors. If you spare the boss, he becomes a contact for future missions but may betray you later. Killing him gives an immediate cash bonus.
