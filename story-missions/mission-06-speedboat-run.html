Speedboat Run

Chapter: Chapter 1: Making Contact · Difficulty: Normal · Reward: $4,000

This mission takes place in Chapter 1: Making Contact and is rated Normal. Completing it rewards you with $4,000.

What You Need to Do

  1. Rendezvous at the marina
  2. Take the speedboat to the offshore exchange point
  3. Fend off the ambush boats
  4. Deliver the cargo to the mainland safehouse

Strategy Guide

Stay in the speedboat — it's faster than the attackers' dinghies. Use the SMG for drive-by shooting from the boat.

Trivia

Boat chases are expected to be a major gameplay element in GTA6, given the Vice City setting with its extensive waterways and coastline.
