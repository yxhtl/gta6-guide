Warehouse Raid

Chapter: Chapter 1: Making Contact · Difficulty: Hard · Reward: $6,500

Walkthrough Steps

  1. Scout the warehouse from the adjacent rooftop
  2. Plant C4 on the three supply crates
  3. Fight through the reinforcements
  4. Escape in the provided getaway car

Trivia

The warehouse district is based on Miami's Wynwood and Allapattah industrial areas, known for their street art and warehouses.

Pro Tips

Bring a rifle for the rooftop scouting phase. Plant C4 on the western crate first — it has the fewest guards nearby.
