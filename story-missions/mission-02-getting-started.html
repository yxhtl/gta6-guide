Getting Started

Chapter: Chapter 1 · Difficulty: Easy · Reward: Free Pistol

This mission takes place in Chapter 1 and is rated Easy. Completing it rewards you with Free Pistol.

What You Need to Do

  1. Pick up the package from the trunk.
  2. Drive to the safehouse.
  3. Avoid police attention on the way.

Strategy Guide

Use back alleys to lose wanted level quickly.
