Convoy Ambush

Chapter: Chapter 4: The Big Time · Difficulty: Hard · Reward: $22,000

This mission takes place in Chapter 4: The Big Time and is rated Hard. Completing it rewards you with $22,000.

What You Need to Do

  1. Set up on the highway overpass with your crew
  2. Stop the lead escort vehicle (EMP device or sniper)
  3. Take out the reinforcement trucks
  4. Secure the target truck
  5. Drive it to the chop shop before the timer runs out

Strategy Guide

Disable the lead vehicle first — the entire convoy stops dead without it. Taking out the helicopter early prevents it from radioing for overwhelming backup.

Trivia

The presence of military-grade hardware like EMP devices in civilian missions suggests a more militarized late-game, similar to GTA Online's escalation in its later updates.
