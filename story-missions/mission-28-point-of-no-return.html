Point of No Return

Chapter: Chapter 5: Endgame · Difficulty: Hard · Reward: $0 (story-critical)

Mission Objectives

  1. Meet with your crew one last time
  2. Choose your endgame approach — Option A: eliminate the FIB contact (stealth)
  3. Option B: rob the Federal Reserve (loud assault)
  4. Option C: play both sides against each other (most complex)
  5. Complete the first phase of your chosen path

Tips

Make a manual save before choosing — this is the point of no return. Option C requires the highest crew stats across the board but yields the best outcome for all characters involved.

Trivia

Multiple endings based on choices made throughout the game are strongly rumored for GTA6, with a more nuanced system than GTA5's three-option endgame choice.
