Highway Heist

Chapter: Chapter 2: Rising Heat · Difficulty: Hard · Reward: $12,000

Mission Objectives

  1. Set up the roadblock on the I-95 overpass
  2. Stop the armored truck
  3. Crack the safe (drill minigame under time pressure)
  4. Escape through the side roads
  5. Split the take at the safehouse

Tips

Place the roadblock after the highway bend — the driver won't see it in time to brake. Use sticky bombs on the back doors if the drill takes too long.

Trivia

Highway chases and vehicle takedowns are expected to be a core gameplay loop, building on GTA5's armored car robberies with more cinematic execution.
