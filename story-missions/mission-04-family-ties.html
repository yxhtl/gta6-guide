Family Ties

Chapter: Chapter 2 · Difficulty: Easy · Reward: $800

Mission Objectives

  1. Drive to Lucia's family home in Little Havana.
  2. Defend the house from rival gang attack.
  3. Chase down the fleeing gang leader.

Tips

Take cover behind the sofa for the best firing angle. Enemies come in 3 waves.

Trivia

Little Havana is a returning district from the original Vice City — expect many callbacks.
