The First Score

Chapter: Chapter 1 · Difficulty: Normal · Reward: $2500

Mission Objectives

  1. Meet Jason at the diner.
  2. Scout the target location.
  3. Choose your approach: stealth or loud.
  4. Execute the heist.
  5. Lose the cops and return to safehouse.
Stealth approach: enter through the back door after 10pm. Guards have a shift change at midnight.

Trivia

Mission introduces the heist preparation system — your first taste of GTA6's expanded planning mechanics.
