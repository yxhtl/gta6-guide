Storm the FIB Building

Chapter: Chapter 5: Endgame · Difficulty: Very Hard · Reward: $35,000

Walkthrough Steps

  1. Infiltrate the FIB headquarters downtown
  2. Plant the virus in the server room on floor 12
  3. Download the evidence files
  4. Destroy the physical backup servers
  5. Fight through the FIB tactical response teams
  6. Escape via the helicopter on the roof

Trivia

The FIB (GTA universe's version of the FBI) has been a recurring antagonist organization since GTA5, first appearing as a major faction in the series' HD era.

Pro Tips

Bring the heavy sniper for the rooftop section. The server room is on floor 12 — elevators are disabled once the alarm triggers, so take the stairs. Save at least one grenade for the helicopter escort on the roof.
