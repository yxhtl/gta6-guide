First Wheels

Chapter: Prologue · Difficulty: Easy · Reward: $800 + Compact Car

Mission Objectives

  1. Steal a car from the dealership lot
  2. Lose the heat (2-star wanted level)
  3. Deliver the car to the garage

Tips

Use alleyways to break line of sight quickly. Don't shoot — just drive.

Trivia

Rockstar traditionally names the protagonist's first car something memorable. This mission establishes the player's starter vehicle.
