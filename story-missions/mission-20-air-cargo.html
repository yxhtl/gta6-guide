Air Cargo

Chapter: Chapter 3: Power Struggle · Difficulty: Hard · Reward: $15,000

Mission Objectives

  1. Drive to Vice City International Airport
  2. Steal the cargo plane from the hangar
  3. Fly to the drop zone (stay below radar altitude)
  4. Parachute the cargo to the ground team
  5. Land the plane at the desert airstrip

Tips

Fly below 500ft to avoid air patrol radar detection. The cargo drop must land within the yellow circle or you'll have to retrieve it manually from the swamp.

Trivia

Aircraft smuggling missions return, with GTA6's map rumored to include multiple airstrips and a much larger flyable airspace than previous games.
