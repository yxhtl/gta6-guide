PC Players: Wait or Jump In?

Rockstar historically releases PC versions 6-18 months after consoles:

  • GTA5: PS3/360 (Sep 2013) → PC (Apr 2015) — 19 months gap
  • RDR2: PS4/Xbox One (Oct 2018) → PC (Nov 2019) — 13 months gap

Estimated GTA6 PC release: Q3-Q4 2027. If you only have a PC, it's worth the wait — Rockstar's PC ports consistently deliver better graphics and higher framerates than console versions. If you have both a console and a PC, buy the console version first to experience launch day, then upgrade when the PC version drops.

Is the Collector's Edition Worth It?

Both GTA5 and RDR2 Collector's Editions sold out fast after launch, with secondary market markups of 50%-200%. If you:

  • Are a die-hard GTA fan / collector → go for it immediately, don't hesitate or it's gone
  • Only want the in-game content, don't care about physical items → the Digital Deluxe is enough
  • Have a tight budget → get the Standard Edition + wait for DLC discounts later

FAQ

When am I charged for a pre-order?

Digital: PlayStation Store and Microsoft Store typically charge immediately at pre-order. Some retailers (Amazon, Best Buy) charge when the item ships.

Can I cancel a pre-order?

PlayStation Store: Cancel before release date (contact support or use the cancel pre-order feature). Microsoft Store: Cancel up to 10 days before release, not within the final 10 days. Amazon: Cancel anytime before shipment. GameStop: In-store deposits are refundable.

Do pre-order bonus codes expire?

Generally, pre-order bonus codes are redeemable for 1-2 years after launch. However, some retailers may require pre-ordering before release to qualify. It's recommended to pre-order between June 25 and November 18.

How much storage space will GTA6 need?

Not yet officially confirmed. For reference: GTA5 launched at ~65GB (later bloated to 110GB+), RDR2 was ~150GB. A conservative estimate for GTA6 is 150GB-200GB. PS5 users should reserve at least 200GB of free space. Xbox Series S users may need an expansion card.

Is there Early Access?

Rockstar never does early access (the kind where Deluxe Edition gets to play 3 days early). Deluxe/Collector's and Standard editions all unlock on the same day. Don't fall for third-party sites claiming otherwise.
