About GTA6 Guide

About GTA6 Guide — a fan-made resource for Grand Theft Auto VI. Pre-order info, cheats, missions, weapons, vehicles, money tips, and more.

Last updated: June 28, 2026

What This Site Is

GTA6 Guide is a fan-made resource for Grand Theft Auto VI. We cover everything from pre-order details and cheat codes to full mission walkthroughs, weapon stats, vehicle catalogs, money-making guides, and collectible locations.

The site is maintained by one person and updated regularly as new information becomes available — especially around launch events, trailers, and game updates.

What We Cover

  • Pre-Order Guide — Editions, prices, bonuses, platform availability
  • Story Missions — Full walkthroughs with tips, time estimates, and rewards
  • Cheat Codes — Reference cheats from GTA5 and confirmed GTA6 codes (updated at launch)
  • Weapons — Stats, locations, and recommendations
  • Vehicles — Full catalog with performance data
  • Money Guide — Best earning methods: heists, stock market, side businesses
  • Collectibles — Locations and rewards
  • Online — Multiplayer tips and updates (coming soon)
